Job Title: Senior Data Engineer

Location: Boston, MA

Company Description:
Join a leading organization that is at the forefront of technological innovation in data engineering. Our commitment to excellence has driven us to seek the most talented individuals to join our team. We are deeply involved in healthcare research and financial solutions, delivering cutting-edge applications that make a significant impact in various industries.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Data Engineer to take a leadership role in our Data Engineering team. The ideal candidate will bring a strong technical expertise in data systems and a proven track record of implementing effective data solutions that support large-scale and diverse operational needs.

Responsibilities:

Design and implement robust data ingestion and integration processes to enhance data accessibility and quality.
Lead the development and optimization of our data architecture, employing advanced database management systems like Snowflake, Neo4j, and BigQuery.
Develop and optimize ETL processes to boost data processing speeds and ensure high data quality.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to build scalable data models from multiple data sources, significantly reducing data processing times.
Utilize graph-based data structures and algorithms to improve query execution speeds and analytical capabilities.
Implement and maintain data security measures and governance protocols to protect data integrity.
Drive innovations in data visualization and data-driven decision making to enhance business operations and customer satisfaction.
Actively participate in the continuous improvement of our continuous integration and delivery pipeline, optimizing software development and deployment processes.
Qualifications:

Master’s degree in Data Architecture, Computer Science, or a related field.
Minimum of 3 years of experience as a Data Engineer with demonstrated experience in a research or healthcare setting.
Strong proficiency in programming languages such as Python, SQL, Scala, and Java.
Extensive experience with data platforms and tools including Docker, Jenkins, Kafka, Git, dbt, and Spark.
Experience with database and cloud technologies like Oracle, Postgres, AWS, GCP, Airflow, and Databricks.
Proven ability to lead data integration and architecture improvements in a complex, multi-source environment.
Certification in Scrum Development and a strong background in project management and team leadership.
Additional Skills:

Excellent problem-solving abilities and strong analytical skills.
Strong communication and collaboration skills to effectively interact with all levels of the organization and foster a cooperative work environment.
Innovative and proactive in proposing solutions and improvements.
Benefits:

Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package.
Opportunities for professional growth and advancement.
Engaging and supportive work environment.
Access to cutting-edge technology and tools.
